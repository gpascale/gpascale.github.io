About

Combinator was formed when childhood friends, and partners in rhythm Sean Fairchild and Isaac Chirino hooked up with guitarist wunderkind Greg Pascale in early 2013. It was a perfectly funky match from day one.

Greg Pascale

Guitar, Vocals

Info

Greg Pascale, originally from New York, has been playing guitar since he was 12 years old and finally learned how to sing a little.

Sean Fairchild

Bass, Lead Vocals

Info

Sean Fairchild is many things. A great bass player, pretty cool guy, but above all a sucker of many cocks.

Isaac Chirino

Percussion, Vocals

Info

Isaac "El Cubano" Chirino may only be Combinator's drummer, but he also knows what chords are.
